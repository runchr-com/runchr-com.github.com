pm-skills-gemini

AI-powered Product Management workflows for Gemini CLI. Structured discovery, strategy, PRD, and launch workflows — directly from the command line.

View on GitHub ↗

Overview

pm-skills-gemini adapts the pm-skills framework into Gemini CLI workflows. Instead of manually crafting prompts, teams can run reusable product workflows with a shared structure.

Who it's for

Product managers, builders, indie hackers, and teams who want more consistent thinking across discovery, strategy, documentation, and launch planning.

Key features

  • 65 PM skills from the pm-skills ecosystem
  • 36 workflow commands for Gemini CLI
  • Project-local installation with .gemini
  • Reusable discovery, strategy, PRD, and launch flows

Why it matters

Most AI tools generate text. Product teams need structure. pm-skills-gemini helps turn AI from a prompt tool into a workflow engine for product work.

Example workflows

  • /discover for product discovery
  • /strategy for product strategy
  • /write-prd for PRD creation
  • /plan-launch for go-to-market planning

Outcome

Better product thinking, clearer documents, and more repeatable workflows across teams.
