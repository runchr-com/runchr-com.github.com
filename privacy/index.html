Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how runchr handles information across products and services operated by runchr, including DocPublish.

Last updated: March 2026

Overview

runchr builds software products and workflows. This policy describes how we handle information that is necessary to operate those services, including account identification, publishing usage, and related operational data.

What we collect

  • Account identifiers such as email address
  • Basic product usage information needed to operate service limits and plan access
  • Technical information required for delivery, performance, and security
  • Support or upgrade request information that you voluntarily submit

How DocPublish works

DocPublish is designed to help users publish from Google Docs to supported destinations. To operate the service, runchr may process account email addresses, publishing quota data, and image delivery data required for publishing workflows.

runchr does not use customer document content for advertising purposes.

How we use information

  • To operate the product and deliver requested functionality
  • To manage free and paid plan access
  • To maintain service reliability, security, and abuse prevention
  • To respond to support, upgrade, or account-related requests

Sharing

runchr does not sell personal data. Information may be shared only when necessary to operate service infrastructure, comply with legal obligations, or protect the integrity and security of the service.

Data retention

We retain information only for as long as needed to operate the service, manage access, resolve support issues, comply with legal obligations, and protect the service from abuse.

Security

We take reasonable measures to protect service data and account-related information. However, no system can guarantee absolute security.

Your choices

You may contact us to request account-related support or ask questions about your data. Where applicable, we will respond within a reasonable time.

Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at support@runchr.com.
